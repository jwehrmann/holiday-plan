How to use the table: tick a route (or select anywhere on its row) to compare its start on the map. The four live totals follow the selected rows; with no selection, they follow the currently visible filtered rows. Selections stay active even when filters hide a row. Select a heading to sort and use the second header row to filter any column. Numeric filters accept expressions such as >10 or <=500; duration filtering and sorting use the midpoint of a displayed range. The name button switches between exact AllTrails titles and itinerary planning names, and that choice is saved locally. The small provider icons beside each title open its route references; green, amber and rose outlines mean high, medium and low route-match confidence. Hover or focus an icon for the exact source title and figures. Length, gain, grade and rating use the linked AllTrails route; multi-link rows total the complete source routes. Duration uses the AllTrails estimate when available, otherwise the itinerary estimate; “all day” is represented as 8–10 hours for aggregation and labelled as an itinerary estimate. Shorter itinerary turnarounds remain documented in the row note. Map pins marked approximate are for spatial planning only—not turn-by-turn navigation. Source figures and start pins were checked July 11, 2026. Recheck road.is, safetravel.is, weather, closures, and each live trail page before setting out.
